4th of July

It’s almost that time of year again! What better way to enjoy the fireworks show then to experience one of our signature Summer flavors.

Fireworks begin at 10pm.

Starting at 6pm, we’ll have a cart in Thomas Square Park, as well as one at the Plaza of the Fountains.

Make sure to come to the Plaza early for burgers and hot dogs from one of our favorite restaurants in town, Ketchup With You Later.

Beat The Heat Rally

It’s another Food Truck Rally! The 4th Annual Beat The Heat Food Truck Rally is Saturday, August 6th, from 5-9pm.

Come down to the Plaza of the Fountains for the finest food trucks in the area. Bring the whole family for a great variety of affordable food and wonderful music!

We’ll have our ice cream cart on the South end of the plaza. Bring your pooch to recieve $1 off a double scoop and a free doggie ice cream!

Our 75th Birthday

Saturday, August 20th, from 11am-3pm we’ll be at Lake Walton to celebrate our 75th year of operation! Join us on the South shore for fun for the whole family.

We’ll have games, food, music, prizes, and of course lots of ice cream! We look forward to seeing you there!

Summer’s End Bash

The 10th Annual End of Summer Bash will be on Saturday, September 10th. This year the event is being moved from Thomas Square Park, to Sweetland Park to celebrate Sweetland’s Grand Opening.

Come out for loads of food, fun, and games. We’ll be stationed on the East entrance, near 4th Avenue.

It’s a great way to wrap up the Summer before sweater weather begins to set in.
